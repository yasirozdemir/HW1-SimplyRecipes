Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries and Brie


Wow our taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions. tart cranberries, and cream brie. It will become our new favorite holiday starter!

Fresh Canberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce


Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour

  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast

  • 1 1/2 teespoons salt

  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive o1, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions. thinly sliced

  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar

  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top

  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cube (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)

  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.
